## Initiating Cloud Data Collection

You can collect and analyze data from your public cloud environments. You can configure a connection from Densify to your cloud-hosted environments through the [cloud connection wizard](https://www.densify.com/docs/WebHelp_Densify_Cloud/Content/Densify_Com/Using_the_Public_Cloud_Connection_Wizard) or through the Densify API.
