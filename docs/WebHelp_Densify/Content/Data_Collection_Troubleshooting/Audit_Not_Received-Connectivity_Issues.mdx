---
title: "Audit Not Received-Connectivity Issues"
---

### Event: Audit has not been received in 2 or more days

This may be due to lack of connectivity with the vCenter or any of the following issues.

<Accordion title="Cause: Connectivity to the vCenter Server">
Solution: Complete the following steps to investigate your vCenter connection:

1. Open the Densify Connector.
2. Select the audit.
3. Click Click to Verify vCenter Connection.

* If the connection is OK, then click Save Connection and Collect Data Now to collect the workload data.
* If an error is returned from the verification step, then investigate the issue on the vCenter. If you need further assistance, contact [Support@Densify.com](mailto:Support@Densify.com).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cause: Problem with the vCenter , e.g. offline">
Solution: Contact your vCenterr administrator.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cause: Problem with your vCenter credentials">
Solution: Ensure that your vCenter credentials are still valid.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cause: The vCenter API subsystem is not responding">
Solution: Contact your vCenter administrator.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cause: Performance/location of the Densify Connector">
Solution: Ensure the connector is in the same timezone as the vCenters and is physically close to the vCenter.
</Accordion>
