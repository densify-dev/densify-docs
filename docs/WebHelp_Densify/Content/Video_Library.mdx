From this page you can browse a collection of informative video content covering features, concepts and procedures.

These videos cover a wide range of topics and are grouped into categories.

Use the arrows on the video slider to view available videos in each category. Select a video, click the Play button on the video to view the topic within the online help. The topic containing the video opens in a new window.

Video content is provided for the following:

* [Using the Densify Connector](#using-the-densify-connector)
* [Viewing Private Cloud Reports](#viewing-private-cloud-reports)
* [Using Supply and Demand Features](#using-supply-and-demand-features)
* [Using the Control Console](#using-the-control-console)

This page does not contain the complete list of available videos in the online help. To see a complete list of videos, enter "video" in the search bar.
