The Control Console and Recommendations streamline infrastructure management processes by communicating status and details of the recommended changes required to optimize your infrastructure and mitigate risk in accordance with your defined policies.

The execution of recommended actions can be automated when integrated into third-party management systems. Action Automation is available for VMware environments. In most organizations recommendations to change infrastructure or move VMs must be planned, approved and then committed and routed to the appropriate destination. Action plans model this workflow.
