### Event: Partial or No Workload Data

More than 90% of the entities have no workload data or more than 90% have partial data.

<Accordion title="Cause: The system may be stopped, terminated or is offline.">
Solution: No action required or remove the entity, if desired.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Cause: The system is an AWS ASG with no children">
Solution: No action required or remove the entity, if desired.
</Accordion>

If none of the above issues apply to your environment, contact Support@Densify.com to review your connectivity details and receive further guidance.
